IMO 2011 Problem 4

Problem: Given n weights of weight 20, 21, ..., 2n-1, place them on a balance one after another such that the right pan is never heavier than the left pan. Determine the number of ways this can be done.

Answer

(2n-1)!! = 1 · 3 · 5 · ... · (2n-1) = (2n)!/(2ⁿ · n!)

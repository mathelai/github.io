IMO 2018 Problem 4

Problem: A site is any point (x,y) where x and y are positive integers from 1 to 20 (400 sites total).

Amy (Red) places stones such that no two red stones are at distance √5 apart. Ben (Blue) places stones anywhere.

Goal: Find the greatest k such that Amy can ensure she places at least k red stones, no matter how Ben plays.

Key insight: Distance √5 = knight's move in chess (e.g., 1 horizontal + 2 vertical, or 2 horizontal + 1 vertical).

Answer

k = 100

The greatest k such that Amy can ensure she places at least k red stones is 100.
